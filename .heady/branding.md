## Color Scheme (PowerShell)
- **Cyan** - Headers, banners, protocol names
- **Green** - Success messages
- **Yellow** - Warnings, phase indicators
- **Red** - Errors
- **Magenta** - Special operations, improvements
- **DarkGray** - Skipped/inactive items
